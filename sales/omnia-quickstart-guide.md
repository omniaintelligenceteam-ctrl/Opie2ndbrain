## Step 2: Upload a Property Photo (1 minute)

1. Click **"New Mockup"** or the **+** button

## Step 3: Add Fixtures (3-5 minutes)

1. From the fixture library, drag fixtures onto the photo
2. Position them where you'd install them
3. Adjust fixture type if needed:
   - **Uplights** ‚Äî For trees, architectural features
   - **Path lights** ‚Äî For walkways, driveways
   - **Well lights** ‚Äî For in-ground installations
   - **Flood lights** ‚Äî For large area coverage
   - **Wall wash** ‚Äî For fences, walls

### üéØ Placement Tips

| Feature | Recommended Lighting |
|---------|---------------------|
| Large trees | 2-3 uplights from different angles |
| Small trees/bushes | 1-2 uplights |
| Walkways | Path lights every 8-10 feet |
| Architectural features | 1-2 uplights or wall wash |
| Entrance | Path lights + accent lighting |
| Patio/deck | Downlighting or perimeter |

**Pro tip:** Less is often more. Start conservative ‚Äî you can always add fixtures.

## Step 4: Generate the Mockup (1-2 minutes)

1. Click **"Generate Mockup"**
2. Wait for AI to process (usually 30-60 seconds)
3. Review the nighttime visualization

### What You'll See
- Realistic nighttime scene
- Light cones from your fixtures
- Ambient glow and shadows
- Property fully transformed

### Not Happy with Results?

1. Adjust fixture positions
2. Add or remove fixtures
3. Regenerate

**Pro tip:** The first generation is usually 90% right. Minor adjustments fix the rest.

## Step 5: Create the Quote (2-3 minutes)

1. Click **"Create Quote"** from your mockup
2. Review the fixture list (auto-populated)
3. Add your pricing:
   - Per-fixture pricing, or
   - Custom line items
4. Add notes or terms
5. Review total

### Quote Customization

| Field | What to Include |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Line items** | Each fixture with quantity and price |
| **Labor** | Installation cost |
| **Materials** | Wire, transformers, connectors |
| **Notes** | Timeline, warranty, payment terms |
| **Terms** | Your standard contract terms |

## Step 6: Send to Customer (1 minute)

1. Click **"Send Quote"**
2. Enter customer's email
3. Add a personal message (optional)
4. Send!

### What the Customer Receives

**Pro tip:** Follow up within 24 hours. "Did you get a chance to look at the mockup?" converts well.
